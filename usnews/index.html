David Sundland is a web developer based in Washington, DC. His passions include analytic puzzles, exploration, live music, non-motorized transportation, beer, and the Oxford comma. His first foray into web development was a self-taught project providing a comprehensive live music calendar for DC, using WordPress for the front end and Google Sheets as a makeshift CMS and HTML generator. The thrill of computer-based problem solving prompted him to dive headlong into studying the full stack.

He possesses great attention to detail, strong analytic and problem-solving abilities, & the ability to organize and simplify complex information. His skills include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Ajax, REST APIs, Python, Node.js, RegEx, Git, GitHub, React, Redux, MongoDB, and TDD with Mocha and Chai.
