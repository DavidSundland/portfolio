About Me

Hello! I am a Web Developer whose passions include analytic puzzles, exploration, live music, non-motorized transportation, beer, and the Oxford comma. My first foray into web development was a self-taught project providing a comprehensive live music calendar for DC, using WordPress for the front end and Google Sheets as a makeshift CMS and HTML generator. The thrill of computer-based problem solving prompted me to dabble in a lot of self-study and ultimately to dive headlong into an immersive program studying the full stack.
